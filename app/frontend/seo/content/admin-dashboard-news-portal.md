## Customizing Publication Settings: Volume, Categories & Auto-Publish

The beauty of NewsPortal's admin dashboard lies in its granular control over every aspect of content publication. This level of customization ensures that your news portal reflects your unique editorial voice and serves your specific audience with precision.

### Fine-Tuning Publication Volume

One of the primary challenges in automated news is preventing content overload. The dashboard allows you to easily set and adjust the daily or weekly publication volume. Do you want to publish 50 articles a day or just 10 curated pieces? A few clicks can define your strategy, ensuring your audience receives a consistent, digestible stream of news without feeling overwhelmed. This control helps maintain editorial standards and prevent your portal from becoming a firehose of information.

### Tailoring News Categories

Relevance is key to engaging readers. NewsPortal's dashboard provides robust tools for managing your news categories, such as Technology, Business, World, Health, Sports, and Entertainment. You can select which categories to fetch news for, assign priorities, and even create custom categories to better suit your niche. This meticulous categorization, coupled with [dynamic news filtering](/blog/news-category-filtering-ai) on the frontend, ensures that readers can always find the content most relevant to their interests, thereby enhancing their overall experience.

### Mastering Auto-Publish & Scheduling

The "set it and forget it" mentality can be a double-edged sword without proper oversight. NewsPortal's auto-publish settings provide flexibility and peace of mind. You can enable automatic publication for certain categories or articles, schedule content for specific times, or even pause publication entirely for manual review. This blend of automation and human oversight is crucial for maintaining content quality and brand integrity. For platforms relying on [real-time news aggregation](/blog/real-time-news-aggregation-ai), the ability to schedule publication windows ensures a steady, controlled flow of fresh content.
